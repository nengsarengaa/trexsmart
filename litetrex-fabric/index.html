HOW LITETREX WORKS

LiteTrex® fabric has a water repellent quick-dry layer that keeps rain and wind out over a durable 200 denier oxford weave. It is waterproof to 15,000mm H2O and fire retardant to CPAI-84 standard.

IDEAL FOR

LiteTrex® function fabric is ideal for use in products used in outdoor pursuits such as backpacks, rucksacks and duffels. Plus it is also used in camping products like tents, bivvys, swags, and bedrolls in areas where breathability is not required. It’s functionality also means it has numerous applications in emergency services, disaster and the military uses.
